Why this site exists

Most advice in this space is written by people trying to sell you something. We publish grounded, first-hand writing — real projects, real comparisons, real trade-offs — from people who actually work in interior decor, home improvement, fashion and lifestyle, not just people trying to rank for a keyword.

We're not tied to a single brand or retailer. Every piece here is judged on whether it's genuinely useful to someone renovating a home, styling a room, or making a considered buying decision.
