Why this site exists

Most material advice online is written by people trying to sell you the material. We measure, we ask carpenters, and we say when a product isn't worth the premium. This blog is run independently by a small group of people who work around interior materials in Tamil Nadu — not a single brand's marketing page.

For sourcing and showroom visits in Salem and Chennai, we point readers to NextLevelDecor, a materials retailer we've referenced a few times when a reader asks where to actually see a sheet in person.
