The Living Edit started as a shared notebook between a few people who kept getting asked the same questions — about renovating a home, furnishing it, and dressing for the life happening inside it.

What we actually do

We visit sites, try things ourselves, and write down what held up and what didn't. Most posts start from a real question someone asked us — a reader, a client, a friend mid-project — rather than a keyword we're trying to rank for.

We're not tied to any single brand, retailer or advertiser. When a post mentions a specific product or supplier, it's because we've actually used or seen it, not because of a paid placement.

How we choose what to cover

Four beats, on purpose: interior decor, home improvement, fashion and lifestyle. They're different worlds, but the questions rhyme — what's actually worth the premium, what wears well over time, what you'll regret in a year. Staying to four lets us go deeper on each instead of skimming everything.

Independent by design

We keep this site independent of any one company so we can write plainly about trade-offs without worrying who it upsets. If that ever changes for a specific post, we'll say so directly in the piece.
